    1.1)chickpea is a short herbaceous annual, 20 to 70 cm tall

    1.2)chickpea has an extensive root system the taproot often penetrates well below one metre

2)PURPLE FLOWERING TYPE

    2.1)chickpea flowering is indeterminate, extending to 60 days or more single, small purple or white flowers are produced in axillary racemes and are highly self pollinated

    2.2)the fruit is an inflated pod containing between two and four ovules

3)KABULI AND DESI CHICKPEA SEED

    3.1)chickpea seed weighs from 200 to 500 gm per 1000 seeds kabuli seeds are much larger than desi seeds

    3.2)although seeds always possess the distinctive chickpea beak , they vary considerably in shape and colour:

        3.2.1)kabuli types have larger, more rounded cream-coloured seeds

        3.2.2)desi types have relatively small, angular seeds with rough, usually yellow to brown seed coats
